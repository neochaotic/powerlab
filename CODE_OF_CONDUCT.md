# Contributor Code of Conduct

PowerLab follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/). Be civil, technical-first, and assume good intent. Discrimination, harassment, sustained disruption, doxing, or threats are out of scope here and will get you removed without ceremony.

Reports go to the maintainer at the email in `SECURITY.md`. The reporter's identity is kept confidential.
