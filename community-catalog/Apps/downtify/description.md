Downtify is a **self-hosted web app** that downloads music from Spotify — without touching the Spotify API, without needing an account, and without any Premium subscription. Just drop a link and get a fully-tagged audio file. It resolves track metadata directly from Spotify's public embed pages, finds the best audio match on YouTube Music, downloads it with , converts it with , and embeds album art + all metadata with .
