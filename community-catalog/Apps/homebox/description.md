With a focus on simplicity and ease of use, Homebox is the perfect solution for your home inventory, organization, and management needs. While developing this project, We've tried to keep the following principles in mind: - 🧘 _Simple but Expandable_ - Homebox is designed to be simple and easy to use. No complicated setup or configuration required. But expandable to whatever level of infrastructure you want to put into it. - 🚀 _Blazingly Fast_ - Homebox is written in Go, which makes it extremely fast and requires minimal resources to deploy.
